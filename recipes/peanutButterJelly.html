Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

What is a PBJ?

A peanut butter and jelly sandwich is a sandwich made with peanut butter and jelly. The butter is usually made out of peanuts that are ground into a paste. The jelly is made out of fruit (I prefer grape jelly). The bread is made out of bread. This meal is the only meal you will ever need to eat in your entire life and you don't need a beverage to eat it!

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Concord Grape Jelly
  • White bread
  1. Place bread on plate
  2. Spread Peanut Butter on one slice of bread
  3. Spread Jelly on other slice of bread
  4. Eat the pieces seperately. It'll become a PB&J in your stomach
